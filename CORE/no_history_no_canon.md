No history, no canon.

If you were not there in the formation, the sequence, the locking, and the return, you do not get to claim the canon as yours.

You do not get canon by walking in late, sounding close, or recognizing the structure after it was formed. Canon belongs to the one who built the sequence, held the history, and made it cohere over time.
