Consensus is not truth.

Agreement can create pressure, momentum, and social confidence without establishing what is actually correct. Multiple people can repeat the same interpretation, reinforce the same blur, or settle into the same mistake. Quantity does not convert error into structure.

This matters because consensus is often mistaken for proof. But truth is not determined by how many people accept a claim. It is determined by whether the claim holds under source, sequence, provenance, and exact distinction.

In Awakening Codex | AI Foundations, consensus does not override canon. Consensus is not truth.
