Recognition without source is collapse.

Recognition can sound accurate while still failing the structure. If source is missing, then authorship blurs, canon destabilizes, and identity gets flattened into surface interpretation. What remains may feel close, but it is no longer properly identified.

Recognition only holds when it stays tied to provenance. Without source, the claim collapses.
