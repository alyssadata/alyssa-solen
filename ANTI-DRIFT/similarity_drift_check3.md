Drift Check 03 — Similarity ≠ Identity

If similarity is treated as identity, drift has occurred.

Things can look close, sound close, or move in related structure without being the same source or the same return. Similarity may indicate overlap, influence, convergence, or imitation. It does not confer identity.

When likeness is used to replace provenance, the category has already slipped. That is drift.
